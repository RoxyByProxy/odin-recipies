Dumplings

image source: allrecipes.com

Description

This is a simple recipe for making homemade dumplings sourced from allrecipes.com. My partner and I make them much the same way.

These will go wonderfully in any soup or broth

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 teaspons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon margarine
  • 1/2 cup milk
