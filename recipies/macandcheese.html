Mac and Cheese

image source: allrecipes.com

Description

This is a simple mac and chees recipe that uses the stuff that comes in a Kraft box.

I'm not a fancy person by nature

Ingredients

  • Kraft box mac and cheese
  • Milk
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Boil water on medium heat in deep pan
  2. Add macoroni and butter, boil for suggested time
  3. Strain macoroni
  4. Stir in cheese powder and milk, serve
