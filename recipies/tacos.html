Tacos

Description

This recipie makes ground beef tacos in a simple tex-mex style for 2 or 3 people, it can be made with hard or soft shells and toppings of choice.

This is the taco recipie I use when cooking and focuses on being quick to make without sacrificing taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • Ground red pepper
  • Taco seasoning of choice
  • Hard or soft taco shells

Steps

  1. Thaw ground beef
  2. Brown beef on stovetop at medium in cooking pan
  3. Lower heat to low-medium and add water if pan is dry
  4. Add taco seasoning liberally, red pepper and additional spices to taste
  5. Allow to finish on low while making shells
  6. Hard shells may be microwaved for warmth, soft shells can be tossed in a pan on medium for a few seconds
  7. Add toppings of choice and serve!
