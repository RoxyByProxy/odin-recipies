Dumplings

image source: allrecipes.com

Description

This is a simple recipe for making homemade dumplings. With a focus on speed and ease. My partner and I make them much the same way.

These will go wonderfully in any soup or broth

Ingredients

  • Soup or broth
  • Can of croissant dough

Steps

  1. Split canned dough into pieces as suggested by the can
  2. Drop dough into boiling soup or similar dish
  3. Serve
